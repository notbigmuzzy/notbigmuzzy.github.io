Sound Board
80's styled sound board. I had a lot of fun with scripting this one because of the limitations of HTML5 audio element in which, a loop is available but not very suitable for a non-stop looping of a track. Basically, you always get a small, couple of milliseconds, pause at the end of the loop which is problematic if you need to produce a certain kind of rhythm and maintain BPM of the track.
My solution? Two players in an 'if' loop which load and play the first one, stops for a certain amount of time (the duration of a track), loads the source of the first to the second one and plays it. Rinse and repeat :)
